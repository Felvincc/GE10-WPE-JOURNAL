Introduction

Welcome to my GE10 Website Journal, the following is a compilation for the 5 C's of Silliman. This 1st semester was filled with events that I usually would never join or participate in. But in joining these activities, events, and organizations, I met so many amazing people, forever friends, and discovered some passions I have for some of the entries in this journal. Though it was tiring at time finding the energy to get up and attend some of these events for the sake of compliance, I dont regret any of those action, because I had so much fun listening, participating, or simply just being at the place of the event. I hope you enjoy checking these journal entries out as much as I did doing them in person!
