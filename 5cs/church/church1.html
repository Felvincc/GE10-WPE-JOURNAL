Church Mass 1

Reflection / Description

For this day in church, the priest talked about the usual stuff, but what caught my attention was that he said that we should go explore Buglasan. He said this because it's like the only festival in the region that invites the different municipalities to have a booth to promote their area and sell their local produce. I think it’s important that we support the locals in the different areas and municipalities of our region to help develop their regions as well. This activity made me realize the importance of supporting local shops and vendors, because at the end of the day, its us locals and natives that really help and care for each other in times of need

I believe this entry deserves 1 point, because I attended mass at the Perpetual Help Church.
