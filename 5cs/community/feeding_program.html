FBPC Feeding Program

Reflection / Description

Organized by the FBPC, this event held a little program for kids in a daycare. I was part of the food committee, basically the ones who made and provided food for the children. I had to leave early before the event when we were making the food (champorado) for the children because of a group meeting for another class, but I did contribute to the funds and the cooking of the champorado. The event was fun, I didn’t expect the MC’s to be very good at interacting with children. The children played games like, bring me this item, some variation of musical chairs, and trivia. This activity made me realize I should be more proactive in my beliefs and morals, without this organization, I would have never done this myself.

I believe this enty deserves 2 points, since I was apart of the Food committee, and helped fund and cook the food.
