CHS Exodus Project

Reflection / Description

This event was organized by my CHS group and I. In this little event, our leader, Carlisle organized a bunch of teenagers to come to a chapel. And we told the story of exodus, specifically the part where moses fled and split the red sea. We bought food, drinks, and ice cream, and played games with prizes with the kids. Afterwards we had a bunch of food left, so we drove around sibulan giving them away and interviewing them for our CHS project. I realized a lot in doing this activity, one of the vendors we interviewed talked about how difficult it is bringing money to the family, saying that even if her kids ask for Jolibee, she would still get them Jolibee even though they don't have enough money, because they're just little kids. It was a very eye opening experience.

I believe this entry deserves 3 points, because we collectively as a group organized this little event, we split the workload equally among each other, with Carlisle as the main leader.
